1. Conversation Awareness

American Social Gatherings

In the United States, social gatherings can include potlucks, barbecues, housewarmings, and game nights. Guests are often expected to respond to invitations and sometimes bring food or drinks.

A potluck is a gathering where each guest brings a dish to share. In many informal gatherings, people mingle, catch up, and try to make new guests feel welcome.

Americans often appreciate direct but polite responses. If someone cannot attend, they usually decline kindly and RSVP clearly.
